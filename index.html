STFU

STFU is a series of audio + video performances generated in real-time by Chicago New Media Artists for the fortuitous goal of exploiting the human brain's innate ability to create order from chaos.

STFU invites you to participate by letting us disrupt your internal feedback-loop, to create a pattern that didn't actually exist in the first place.
